STANDARD DELIVERY

ENJOY YOUR NESPRESSO

1. STANDARD DELIVERY IN 48 HOURS

These days, delivery takes 2 working days.

How does it work?

Your order will be delivered within 48 hours*, if you place the order online or by phone until 3 PM on a business day. Please update your Nespresso account information with your phone number, so that we can contact you, and an address at which the package can be received.

OPTIONS AVAILABLE FOR ABOVE DELIVERY METHODS:

Swap recyclable capsules. Learn more about courier recycling (SWAP).

Online payment via bank card or cash on delivery.

FREE DELIVERY for orders of one coffee machine or of total value of minimum 100 RON.

For orders that do not meet the specified criteria, the cost of standard delivery is 15 RON.
