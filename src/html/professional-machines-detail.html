MOMENTO COFFEE 100

Enjoy your favourite coffee with complete peace of mind thanks to the Nespresso Momento Touchless remote control. Nespresso Momento 100 offers 5 black coffee sizes as well as a hot water function, delivering cafe style coffee from an Espresso to an Americano. In addition, with the help of the capsule recognition function, it can provide recommendations for optimal coffee cup length, aromatic coffee notes, and intensity for the selected coffee capsule.
